Adrien Gervaix — 2016 or the year you're looking at it

I’m a 26 years old designer who like to work with hyperlinks, sliders or dropdown menus. These past months I’ve been working on my side project Collect.io and Dartagnan at Bmobile. I’ve worked in different design fields from webdesign to motion design and mainly as part of small agency. I’m focused on giving the easiest experience to the user on all of their devices. I like to research and organise the results to create wireframes and prototypes.
